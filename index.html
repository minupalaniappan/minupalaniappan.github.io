About Me

I'm just a kid trying to make cool stuff.

I'm Minu Palaniappan. I was born in the Bay Area and am currently 18 years old. I enjoy building ideas primarily on the web and have been building awesome ideas since I was 14. I'm currently pursuing a BS in Computer Science at the University of California, Davis.

I've spent primarily most of my time in building full-stack websites with the rapid templating language, Django. I've also experimented with the iOS and Android platforms, releasing a few apps to their respective app stores. In the future, I hope to pursue mobile technologies to expand my versatility.

I'm not all work though. In my free time, I enjoy to binge-watch episodes on Netflix, play casual pick-up basketball, catch-up on my Twitter and Instagram.

Why I co-founded Dojo?

It's group Twitter with the laid-back feel of Yik-Yak

Since the summer of my senior year, I've had the opportunity to work with a close friend on a fresh social network that highlights cutting-edge technologies such as real-time scaleable streaming, Socket-io powered chat across multiple platforms, and our patent pending camera.
